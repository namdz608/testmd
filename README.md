# Huong dan tao tai khoan Shopify Partner de vao man hinh `App distribution > All apps`

Tai lieu nay duoc tong hop de thao tac nhanh vao dung man hinh nhu anh: ben trai co menu `App distribution`, muc con `All apps`.

## 1. Ket qua can dat

Sau khi hoan tat, ban dang nhap duoc vao **Shopify Partner Dashboard** va vao duong dan:

`App distribution` -> `All apps`

Neu tai khoan da co quyen, ban se thay trang quan ly danh sach app trong Partner Dashboard, giong man hinh mau.

## 2. Dieu kien can co

- 1 email chua dang ky hoac co the dung de tao/nhan loi moi Shopify account
- Co the truy cap trang dang ky doi tac Shopify
- Neu dang vao bang tai khoan nhan vien trong cong ty, can chu tai khoan Partner cap quyen phu hop

## 3. Cach tao tai khoan Shopify Partner

### Cach 1: Tao moi tu dau

1. Mo trang dang ky doi tac:
   `https://www.shopify.com/partners`
2. Bam `Become a partner` hoac `Sign up`.
3. Tao Shopify account moi, hoac dang nhap neu da co Shopify account.
4. Lam theo cac buoc tao **Partner account / Partner organization**.
5. Chap nhan cac dieu khoan cua Shopify Partner Program neu he thong yeu cau.
6. Xac thuc email va hoan tat cau hinh bao mat dang nhap neu Shopify bat buoc.
7. Sau khi tao xong, dang nhap vao Partner Dashboard.

### Cach 2: Ban da co Shopify account, chi can vao Partner Dashboard

1. Dang nhap bang Shopify account hien co.
2. Neu Shopify cho phep tao Partner organization moi, tiep tuc tao.
3. Sau khi tao xong, vao Partner Dashboard.

## 4. Cach vao dung man hinh nhu anh

Sau khi dang nhap Partner Dashboard:

1. O menu ben trai, chon `App distribution`.
2. Chon `All apps`.
3. Neu he thong hien the banner `Visit your Dev Dashboard for app building and monitoring` thi do la binh thuong. Shopify da chuyen mot phan build/monitoring sang **Dev Dashboard**; Partner Dashboard van giu phan quan ly phan phoi app va listing.

## 5. Neu khong thay menu `App distribution`

Day la truong hop rat hay gap. Thu lan luot:

1. Kiem tra ban da dang nhap dung **Partner Dashboard**, khong phai Shopify Admin cua store.
2. Neu ban la **owner** cua Partner organization, thu reload hoac dang xuat dang nhap lai.
3. Neu ban la **staff/team member**, yeu cau owner vao:
   `Partner Dashboard` -> `Team`
4. Tai day owner can cap quyen:
   - `Manage apps`
   - Neu can, cac quyen lien quan khac de quan ly app/listing
5. Sau khi duoc cap quyen, dang nhap lai roi kiem tra `App distribution` -> `All apps`.

## 6. Neu muon tao app de hien trong `All apps`

Neu vao duoc trang nhung danh sach trong, do la binh thuong vi tai khoan chua co app nao.

Ban co the:

1. Tao app moi tu **Dev Dashboard** hoac bang Shopify CLI.
2. Chon kieu phan phoi phu hop:
   - `Public distribution`: dua app len Shopify App Store
   - `Custom distribution`: phan phoi app bang link cho store/to chuc phu hop
3. Sau khi app duoc tao trong he thong, quay lai `App distribution` -> `All apps` de xem va quan ly.

Luu y: Theo tai lieu chinh thuc, sau khi chon distribution method thi khong doi lai duoc, nen can chon dung ngay tu dau.

## 7. Mot so luu y de tranh nham lan

- **Shopify Partner Program hien co the tham gia mien phi** o muc co ban.
- Tat ca partner bat dau tu tier `Registered`.
- Nhieu bai viet cu nhac den `Plus Partner`; Shopify da neu ro thuong hieu/doi chuong trinh cu nay da sunset tu **thang 12/2024**.
- Neu muc tieu cua ban la build app, giao dien trong anh la khu vuc quan ly phan phoi app trong Partner Dashboard, con viec build/logs/monitoring hien duoc dieu huong nhieu hon sang Dev Dashboard.

## 8. Link chinh thuc nen mo

- Trang dang ky Shopify Partner: https://www.shopify.com/partners
- Tong quan Shopify Partner Program: https://help.shopify.com/en/partners/about
- Trang quan ly Partner account: https://help.shopify.com/en/partners/manage-account
- Quan ly quyen team trong Partner Dashboard: https://help.shopify.com/en/partners/dashboard/account-access
- Chon kieu phan phoi app: https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/launch/distribution/select-distribution-method

## 9. Checklist nhanh

- Da tao hoac dang nhap Shopify account
- Da tao Partner organization
- Da vao duoc Partner Dashboard
- Da co quyen `Manage apps` neu la staff
- Da mo duong dan `App distribution` -> `All apps`

Neu ban muon, buoc tiep theo minh co the tao them cho ban mot file huong dan thu 2 ve `cach tao app dau tien tren Shopify Partner` ngay trong thu muc nay.
